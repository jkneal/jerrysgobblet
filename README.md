# Jerry's Goblet

A 2-player strategy game based on Gobblet.

## How to Play

**Objective:** Be the first to align 4 of your pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

**Pieces:**
- Each player has 3 stacks of pieces.
- Pieces come in 4 sizes.
- Larger pieces can cover (gobble) smaller pieces.

**On Your Turn:**
1. **Place a Piece:** 
   - Click a stack in your "Hand" (bottom of screen) to select the top piece.
   - Click a square on the board to place it.
   - You can place on an empty square or cover a smaller piece.

2. **Move a Piece:** 
   - Click one of your exposed pieces on the board to select it.
   - Click another square to move it there.
   - You can move to an empty square or cover a smaller piece.

**Gobbling:**
- A larger piece can be placed over or moved onto a smaller piece (yours or opponent's).
- The covered piece is hidden and cannot move until uncovered.
- If a piece is moved, the piece underneath (if any) is revealed.

**Winning:**
The game ends immediately when a player completes a line of 4 pieces of their color.
